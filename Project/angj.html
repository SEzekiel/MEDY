Music and Dance Research paper format 1) Title, course code, date, name of student(s). 2) Objective of the paper 3) Methodology [library and web research, interviews, observation, etc]. 4) Context or history of the area under review 5) Main text 6) Conclusion and/or recommendations 7) Bibliographical references 8)Appendix [if needed] of photos, photo-stats of supporting documents, detailed interviews etc. The document should be professionally formatted, spell-checked, stapled and, if possible, bound
